1.Ratan Tata

key Facts:

Born :28 December 1937 Place of Birth:Mumbai

Early Life

Ratan Tata started his career at Tata Steel in 1962 on the shop floor, shoveling limestone and working alongside blue-collar workers—earning respect the hard way.

Visionary Leadership

He transformed the Tata Group into a global powerhouse, expanding the brand in the international markets.

Personality Traits
  1. Humility and simplicity: Despite his wealth and stature, he remains humble and approachable—often seen at the airport alone, industrial-style.
  2. Visionary Thinking: Innovated through challenges and brought change to the Indian tech world.
